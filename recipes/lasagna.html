Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Make the dough – mix flour, yeast, water, salt, and olive oil; knead and let rise.
  2. Prepare the sauce – cook or blend tomatoes with garlic, oil, and herbs (or use ready-made).
  3. Shape the dough – stretch or roll into a circle.
  4. Add sauce and toppings – spread sauce, then cheese and toppings.
  5. Bake – high heat (220°C/425°F or higher) for 10–15 minutes, until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
