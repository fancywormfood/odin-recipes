Cookies

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Butter
  • eggs
  • baking soda or baking powder
  • vanilla extract
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Cream butter & sugar – beat together until light and fluffy.
  2. Add eggs & vanilla – mix in until combined.
  3. Mix dry ingredients – in a separate bowl, combine flour, baking soda/powder, and a pinch of salt.
  4. Combine wet and dry – mix until a dough forms.
  5. Bake – 175°C (350°F) for 8–12 minutes until edges are golden.
